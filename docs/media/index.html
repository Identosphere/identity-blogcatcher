Last week, FindBiometrics Multimedia Editor Eric Weiss reported live from FTE Global in Las Vegas. The Future Travel Experience conference was bustling with biometrics and identity experts as the aviation industry embraces contactless solutions for passenger processing and security: facial recognition, mobile ID, vaccine passports, and more. Weiss met with these experts, representing the biggest identity vendors in the travel sector, and interviewed them for special episodes of the ID Talk Podcast.

Follow the links below to hear the leading minds in biometrics and aviation discuss crucial industry topics like privacy, security, customer experience, hygiene, and how strong identity technology is going to get us back int he air safely.
