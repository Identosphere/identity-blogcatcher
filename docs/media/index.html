The ID Talk Podcast · Money2020 USA – Hummingbirds AI CEO Nima Schei & CTO Hamed Tabhki

Nima and Hamed introduce us to Guacamole ID, an innovative identity solution that’s perfectly suited for our current era of remote-first work. A continuous authentication technology, Guacamole ID leverages AI and device hardware to ensure only your employees are accessing workstations in real time.
